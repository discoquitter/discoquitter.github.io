About Me

My journey in healthcare started as the Head of Administration at Lilavati Hospital where we built some of Mumbai’s first patient service teams (long before "patient experience" became a buzzword). I then went on to lead the hospital’s international expansion into Kenya.

Later, I scaled patient experience systems across multiple diagnostic centers at NM Medical, adapting to high-volume operations. Over the years, I’ve developed systems that serve everyone from families to high-profile patients like the Dalai Lama, Amitabh Bachchan, and Sachin Tendulkar.

My Philosophy

Hospitals grow when patients trust them enough to return, refer others, and recover faster.

In an industry defined by high stakes and noise, patient experience is one of the few true competitive advantages left. Not a checkbox, but a strategic asset that quietly compounds over time.

I build sustainable, high-impact, patient-centric care models that actually work.

You don't win healthcare by marketing but by earning trust, the kind that people would choose with their lives, not once, but twice.
